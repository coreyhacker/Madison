Multifamily Loans

The Madison Group offers a diverse mix of commercial real estate loans to meet the needs of multifamily property owners. Commercial mortgage loan availability vary depending on state, population, DSC, and LTV. Our goal is to structure your loan to obtain the best rate and terms available in the marketplace. Our lending sources include insurance companies, CMBS, banks, credit unions, HUD, Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac. Loans can be for new purchases, refinances, rehab, or construction.

The Madison Group offers a variety of loan programs for our clients with multifamily commercial property loans over $1,000,000. We partner with you to customize terms to fit your financial goals. Put our experienced team to work for you!

Eligible Multifamily Property Types:

Apartments (5 units and greater)
Mobile Home Communities (Parks)
RV Parks
Student Housing
Senior Housing
Mixed Use – Apartments over retail
Apartment Condos
Townhome complexes
Low income housing tax credit – Section 8 Rents

Loan Program Information

Loan Limits $1,000,000 Minimum
Rates Competitive market pricing. Fixed or floating rates
Terms 5, 7, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35 Year Terms
Amortization 25 to 35 Year Amortization
Prepayment Several options including no prepayment penalty
Recourse Nonrecourse available (subject to standard carve outs)
DSC 1.20 DSC Minimum
LTV 75% – 80% LTV/LTC Maximum
Assumable Yes to a qualified borrower.
Property types Apartments, Affordable, Age-Restricted, Conventional, Garden or High-rise, Military, Low income housing, Manufactured Home Communities, RV Parks
Geographic areas Nationwide
