About Us

The Madison Group

Established in 2001, The Madison Group is an effective, efficient, and ethical broker and consultant of loans for commercial buildings and investment properties. Our commitment is to provide is to provide excellent service and superior capital market expertise and highly competitive commercial loan products. The Madison Group will partner with you secure the best commercial loan to meet your financial goals. Our purchase and refinance loan programs allow you to increase availability of funds, preserve working capital, and reduce mortgage expenses.

We specialize in providing commercial loans for apartments, multifamily, retail, office, industrial, single tenant net lease, self-storage, mobile home parks, and most commercial property types.

Our service area is nationwide with our headquarters located outside of Salt Lake City Utah. We focus on providing loans for clients in the western states, including: Utah, Colorado, Wyoming, Montana, Idaho, Washington and Oregon.

“We partner with successful people that believe commercial real estate is a great way to create and preserve wealth”

The Madison Group has direct access to a variety of commercial loan programs from our lenders, including: life insurance companies, commercial banks, bridge lenders, CMBS, and small local banks and credit unions in “tertiary” markets. Whether you are looking for financing for acquisition/ purchase or refinance, we offer solutions because of our exclusive relationships and products.
